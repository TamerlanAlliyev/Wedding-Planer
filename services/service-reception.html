The Celebration

From the first dance to the last song — the joy, the laughter, and all the details you worked so hard to create.

Reception coverage that tells the full story

The reception is where your personalities and planning really shine. I capture the room before guests arrive — tablescapes, florals, cake, stationery — then move into first dance, toasts, cake cutting, and the dance floor. Every laugh, embrace, and spontaneous moment becomes part of your visual story.

I work in both ambient and low light, using subtle flash when needed so the mood stays natural. You'll get a balanced mix of detail shots, wide room views, and candid guest moments, all edited in my fine-art style so your reception images sit seamlessly alongside ceremony and portraits.

What's included

  • Reception space and detail shots (tables, florals, cake, decor)
  • First dance and key moments (toasts, cake cutting, bouquet)
  • Dance floor energy — candid and dynamic
  • Guest reactions and table conversations
  • Room and atmosphere context (wide and medium shots)
  • Consistent fine-art editing with your full gallery
Details you'll treasure
Your invitations, centerpieces, and cake deserve their own frames. I document them in a clean, editorial way that matches your album.
Dance floor & guests
I capture the energy without being intrusive. You'll see the fun, the tears during toasts, and the moments your friends will talk about for years.
