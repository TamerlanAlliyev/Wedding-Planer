The Sacred Moment

Every vow, every tear, every embrace — captured with discretion and artistry as your story unfolds before the altar.

Ceremony coverage that honors the moment

The ceremony is the heart of your wedding day. My approach is calm and unobtrusive — I move quietly, use natural light whenever possible, and never direct or interrupt. The result is a genuine record of your exchange of vows, the reactions of your loved ones, and the atmosphere you worked so hard to create.

Whether you're in a grand church, a garden arch, or a minimalist venue, I capture multiple angles and a mix of wide shots and intimate close-ups. You'll receive both candid moments and classic formal frames, all edited in my signature fine-art style for a cohesive, timeless album.

What's included

  • Full ceremony coverage from processional to recessional
  • Multiple angles (wide, medium, close-up) for variety
  • Candid and formal shots — no stiff posing
  • Guest reactions and key moments (rings, first kiss, etc.)
  • Venue and decor context shots
  • Consistent fine-art editing to match your full gallery
Unobtrusive presence
I blend into the background so you and your guests can be fully present. No flash during vows unless the venue requires it.
Natural & artificial light
I work with available light first for a soft, cinematic look. When needed, I use subtle supplemental light that feels natural.

Ready to capture your ceremony?

Tell me about your date, venue, and vision. I'll send a custom proposal and we can schedule a call.
