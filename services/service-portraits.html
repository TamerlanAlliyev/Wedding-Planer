Just the Two of You

Golden hour portraits that reflect the quiet intimacy between two people in love. Timeless. Natural. Yours.

Portraits that feel like you

Your wedding day flies by — the portrait session is the one block of time that's entirely about the two of you. I use soft, directional light (golden hour when possible), natural settings, and gentle direction so you can relax and connect. The goal is images that look and feel like your relationship: genuine, romantic, and worth framing.

I blend editorial styling with a documentary eye. We might use your venue's best corners, nearby landscapes, or urban backdrops. You'll get a mix of close-ups, environmental portraits, and candid moments between poses — all edited in my fine-art palette for consistency with your full wedding gallery.

What's included

  • Dedicated portrait time (typically 45–90 min) during your day
  • Golden hour or optimal light timing when possible
  • Natural light mastery and soft supplemental light if needed
  • Editorial-style posing guidance — never stiff or awkward
  • Multiple locations or backdrops within your venue area
  • Fine-art editing to match your complete wedding gallery
Golden hour priority
I plan the timeline around the best light whenever we can. That warm, flattering glow makes every portrait feel magazine-worthy.
Emotion over pose
I give you simple prompts and space to be yourselves. The best frames come from real connection, not rigid posing.
