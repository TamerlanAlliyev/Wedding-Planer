About Elena

My name is Elena Voss, and I'm a fine art wedding photographer based in Vienna, Austria. Over eight years, I've had the privilege of documenting over 320 weddings across Europe, the Mediterranean, and beyond.

My approach is calm, unobtrusive, and deeply present — I become a part of your day, not a director of it. The result is a collection of images that genuinely reflect who you are and how you loved.
